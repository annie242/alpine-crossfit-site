For Golden athletes

Built for people who already move.

Golden draws people who climb at the table mountains, ride Lookout, ski on weekends, and show up to Mines labs during the week. You already train — you're looking for a gym that matches. Alpine is it. Every class is coached by a CF-L2 professional. Programming is written in-house by one of our owners, an active CrossFit Quarterfinals competitor. And you'll find members here who race, ski tour, climb, and compete alongside their gym work.

Golden FAQ

Common questions.

How far is Alpine CrossFit from Golden?
About 12 minutes from downtown Golden and Colorado School of Mines, a straight shot east on 6th Avenue or up I-70. Alpine is at 12090 West 50th Place, Wheat Ridge.
Do you work with Mines students?
Yes. We have student discounts — reach out for current rates. Our schedule has morning, midday, and evening classes seven days a week, which works around most course and lab schedules.
Do you coach athletes for specific sports?
Yes. Personal training with a CF-L2 coach can be scoped around specific sports, events, or return-to-sport after injury.
What do memberships cost?
$69 to $999/month, month-to-month, no contracts. Full pricing is on the pricing page.
What if I only want to come two or three times a week?
The 12 Classes Per Month membership ($165) is built for that rhythm.
