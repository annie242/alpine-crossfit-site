Your neighborhood gym

The Applewood CrossFit gym that just happens to sit in Wheat Ridge.

If you live in Applewood, Alpine is your closest CrossFit gym. A few minutes up the road, free parking, and once you're in the door, every class is coached by a CF-L2 professional. We don't chase Denver traffic patterns or I-70 congestion — you're here in under ten minutes from almost anywhere in Applewood.

Our Applewood members cover every age and experience level. Some come once a week to Prime Vitality (the 55+ program at 10 AM Monday/Wednesday/Friday); some train six days. The common thread is that they want to be coached, not left alone on a treadmill.

Getting here

A few minutes, either direction.

Alpine is at 12090 West 50th Place, just off I-70 near Kipling. Most of Applewood is within a five-minute drive — often less.

Applewood FAQ

Common questions.

How close is Alpine CrossFit to Applewood?
A few minutes — most of Applewood is inside a 5-minute drive. Alpine is at 12090 West 50th Place, just off I-70.
Is Alpine in Applewood?
Alpine is technically in Wheat Ridge, but for Applewood residents we're functionally the local gym — closer than most options in either direction.
What's included in the $199 Unlimited membership?
Unlimited CrossFit classes, open gym access, wellness center, Prime Vitality (55+), and 24/7 facility access.
Do you take drop-ins?
Yes — $30 for a single class or $95 for a week pass. For anyone local, the free No Sweat Intro is the better starting point.
What's the age range of your members?
20s through 70s, with a dedicated Prime Vitality program for members 55+ and a wide beginner-friendly class lineup.
