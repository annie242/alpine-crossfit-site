Your hometown CrossFit

The Wheat Ridge gym built for Wheat Ridge.

Alpine has been part of Wheat Ridge since 2010. Our members live and work across every corner of the city — near the Wheat Ridge/Ward light rail station, out through Applewood, down the 38th Avenue corridor, and everywhere in between. You don't drive forty minutes to train with us; you drive five.

What you get at Alpine that you don't at a commercial gym a block closer to home: every class is coached by a CF-L2 professional, the workouts are programmed in-house by one of our owners, and you'll learn every other member's name inside a few weeks.

Who Alpine works for

From first-timers to lifelong athletes.

Our Wheat Ridge members range from people who have never set foot in a gym to active CrossFit competitors. We also run a dedicated Prime Vitality program Monday, Wednesday, and Friday mornings for adults 55+ — the most strength-and-conditioning-oriented 55+ program in Wheat Ridge. While most senior fitness classes stick to low-impact cardio, Prime Vitality teaches barbell lifting, functional movement, and measurable strength progression, scaled appropriately.

If you're a beginner and nervous about walking in, your free No Sweat Intro is 45 minutes with a coach. No class, no pressure.
