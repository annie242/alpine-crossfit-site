Lakewood's coached alternative

Skip past the big-box — this one's coached.

Lakewood has more gyms per square mile than almost any suburb of Denver. You can walk into a big-box closer than Alpine, swipe a card, and get nothing. What you can't do in Lakewood is find another gym where every class — from the 5:30 AM CrossFit to the 10 AM Prime Vitality session for members 55+ — is run by a CF-L2 certified professional.

Our Lakewood members come from across the city: near Belmar, out toward Green Mountain, up by Bear Creek Lake Park, and everywhere between. They make the short drive because what they get when they walk in doesn't exist anywhere closer.

Getting here

A short drive up Kipling.

Alpine is at 12090 West 50th Place in Wheat Ridge, just off I-70 near Kipling — about 10 minutes from Belmar, 12 from Green Mountain, and a similar drive from Bear Creek Lake Park.
