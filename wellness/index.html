What's inside

Four recovery tools, one facility.

Training hard without recovering well is how people stall, burn out, and get hurt. The Alpine Wellness Center is the other half of the equation — the tools your body needs to come back stronger.

Sauna

Heat therapy for cardiovascular conditioning, circulation, and recovery. Regular sauna use is associated with better endurance and faster return from heavy training. Use it post-workout or on rest days.

Cold plunges

Cold immersion for inflammation control, nervous system recovery, and mental clarity. Contrast therapy with the sauna (hot / cold / hot) is one of the most effective recovery protocols available.

Compression boots

Pneumatic compression sleeves that accelerate lymphatic drainage and reduce muscle soreness. Sit back with a book for 20 minutes after a heavy leg day — it works.

Peptide therapy

Targeted peptide protocols for recovery, injury repair, and performance support. By appointment, scoped to the individual during consultation. Ask about current protocols during your No Sweat Intro.

Peptide therapy is administered under appropriate medical supervision. Details on protocols, pricing, and eligibility are discussed during consultation.

Wellness FAQ

Common questions.

What's in the wellness center?
Sauna, cold plunges, compression boots (pneumatic recovery sleeves), and peptide therapy. It's a dedicated recovery and performance space built into the gym.
Is this an open gym?
No. The wellness center is a recovery amenity, not a training floor. Open gym access (for training outside class times) is a separate benefit available to Unlimited members 24/7.
Do I need an appointment to use the sauna or cold plunge?
No. Members can use the sauna, cold plunge, and compression boots any time the wellness center is open. Peptide therapy is by appointment.
Is peptide therapy safe?
Peptide therapy at Alpine is administered under appropriate medical supervision. Every protocol is scoped to the individual during a consultation, including eligibility and safety review.
Can non-members use the wellness center?
Wellness center access is included in every Alpine membership tier. Peptide therapy consultations may be available separately — ask during your free intro.
