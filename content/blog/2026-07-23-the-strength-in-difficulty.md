## The Strength in Difficulty

> Every difficulty in life presents us with an opportunity to turn inward and to invoke our own submerged inner resources. The trials we endure can and should introduce us to our strengths.
>
> — Epictetus, Discourses

That moment when the WOD gets tough, when the weight feels heavy, or the reps seem endless—that's not just physical exertion. It's a crucible, a chance to dig deeper than you thought possible. Epictetus reminds us that these are precisely the moments where we discover what we're truly made of, revealing hidden reserves of grit and resilience.

Don't shy away from the struggle; lean into it. Each challenge in the gym, each setback outside of it, is an invitation to test your mettle and uncover the strength you already possess. Embrace the grind, for it is there you will find your power.

**Today's challenge:** Today, when you hit a wall, mentally or physically, pause for a breath and ask yourself: "What inner resource can I call upon right now?"
