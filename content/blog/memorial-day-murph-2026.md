Every Memorial Day, gyms across the country take on **Murph** — and this year, Alpine is doing it together on **Monday, May 25**.

## What is Murph?

Murph is a hero workout that honors Navy Lieutenant Michael P. Murphy, a Navy SEAL killed in Afghanistan in 2005 and posthumously awarded the Medal of Honor. It was his favorite workout — he called it "Body Armor."

The workout:

- 1-mile run
- 100 pull-ups
- 200 push-ups
- 300 air squats
- 1-mile run

Traditionally done wearing a 20-lb weight vest.

## Everyone is welcome — and yes, that means you

Here's the most important part: **Murph is infinitely scalable.** You do not need to be fit, experienced, or own a weight vest to take part.

- Break the reps up however you like — most people partition them into rounds.
- Scale the movements to your level — ring rows, knee push-ups, assisted squats all count.
- Shorten the runs, or swap them, if a mile isn't your thing yet.
- Skip the vest. It's optional, always.

Whether this is your hundredth Murph or your very first workout, there's a version of this for you. Bring a friend, bring your family, or just come cheer.
