## The True Measure

> If you are distressed by anything external, the pain is not due to the thing itself, but to your estimate of it; and this you have the power to revoke at any moment.
>
> — Marcus Aurelius, Meditations 8.47

That burning sensation in your lungs, the trembling in your legs during a heavy lift, or the clock ticking down on a brutal WOD — these are just physical sensations. They are neutral. It's your mind that labels them as 'painful,' 'impossible,' or 'too hard.' Your perception turns a challenge into suffering.

Today, recognize that the discomfort isn't the enemy; your judgment of it is. You have the power to reframe these moments, seeing them not as obstacles, but as opportunities to prove your resilience. Take control of your internal narrative, and you'll find strength you didn't know you had.

**Today's challenge:** During your toughest moment in today's workout, pause for a breath and consciously rename the feeling from 'pain' to 'effort' or 'growth'.
