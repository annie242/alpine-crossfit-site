## Embrace The Struggle

> No man is more unhappy than he who never faces adversity; for he is not permitted to prove himself.
>
> — Seneca, Moral Letters to Lucilius 13.4

Think about the moments in a WOD when you want to quit. The barbell feels too heavy, your lungs are burning, and every muscle screams. This isn't just physical pain; it's adversity, a test of your mental fortitude. It's in these exact moments that you truly discover what you're made of.

Without these challenges, you'd never know your limits or how much stronger you can become. Each struggle is an opportunity to prove your resilience, not just to others, but to yourself. So, instead of dreading the hard parts, lean into them; they are forging your character.

**Today's challenge:** Today, when you hit that wall in your workout, acknowledge the discomfort, then push through for just one more rep or one more second than you think you can.
