## A Test of Character

> No man is more unhappy than he who never faces adversity, for he is not permitted to test himself.
>
> — Seneca, Moral Letters to Lucilius 13.4

That moment when the WOD feels impossible, when your lungs burn and your muscles scream – that's not just a physical challenge. It’s a test. A test of your resolve, your patience, and your commitment to showing up, even when it hurts. It’s in these moments of adversity that we truly discover what we're made of.

Without these trials, we'd remain untested, unaware of our own strength. Embrace the discomfort, not as punishment, but as an opportunity to prove to yourself that you are capable of more than you think. You are building character with every rep, every run, every time you choose to push through.

**Today's challenge:** Today, when you hit that wall, acknowledge the discomfort, then choose one more rep, one more meter, or one more minute than you thought possible.
