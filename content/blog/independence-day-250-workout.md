America turns **250** this year, and Alpine is throwing the workout it deserves. Here's everything happening at the gym around the holiday: the workout, the weekend hours, and the t-shirts.

## The 250 Workout — Friday, July 3

We'll celebrate America's 250th in classes on **Friday, July 3**. A partner workout, ~45 minutes, with the numerology of 1776 and 250 baked into every part.

**45 minutes. Two parts.**

### Part 1 — Row the cargo boat, dodge the tea tax

- **Partner row 1,776 meters**
- **EMOM:** one partner throws a tea bag overboard (a bean-bag toss).
- **Miss the bag → pay the tax of 250.** A 250 (20-ft) wall-ball lunge down and back, within that same minute.

### Part 2 — The 13 original colonies

13 rounds. I-go / you-go by movement. Each round:

- **1** deadlift @ 250 / 177.6 lb
- **7** wall balls
- **7** pull-ups
- **6** burpees over the erg

**1 – 7 – 7 – 6** reps. That's 1776, baked into every round.

## Why these numbers

In case it looked random — it isn't.

- **1,776 m row** — 1776, the year.
- **13 rounds** — the 13 original colonies.
- **1 – 7 – 7 – 6 reps** — 1, 7, 7, 6 → 1776.
- **250 / 177.6 lb deadlift** — 250th anniversary; 177.6 = 1776 ÷ 10.
- **250-ft "tea tax" lunge** — the 250th.
- **"Cargo boat" + "tea bag overboard"** — Boston Tea Party, naturally.

Yes, Megan thought about it that hard.
