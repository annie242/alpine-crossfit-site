## Build Your Inner Strength

> No man is free who is not master of himself.
>
> — Epictetus, Discourses 4.1.1

True freedom isn't about having no rules or obligations. It's about having mastery over your own desires, impulses, and reactions. When you can control your mind, your body, and your choices, you're not a slave to external circumstances or fleeting cravings.

This self-mastery is built through consistent effort, just like your physical strength. Every rep, every difficult conversation, every moment you choose discipline over indulgence, you are forging that inner freedom. Today, recognize that real power comes from within you.

**Today's challenge:** Before your next set, pause and consciously choose to push through the discomfort, reminding yourself that you are the master of your effort, not its victim.
