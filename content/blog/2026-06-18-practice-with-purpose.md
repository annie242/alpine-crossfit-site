## Practice With Purpose

> Every habit and faculty is corroborated and intensified by the corresponding actions, the habit of walking by walking, the habit of running by running.
>
> — Epictetus, Discourses 2.18.1

Think about that muscle-up, that heavy clean, or even just showing up consistently. Each rep, each session, each choice reinforces the habit. You aren't just building strength or skill; you're forging the very character that makes those achievements possible.

This isn't about perfection, but about intention. What you do repeatedly, you become. So, approach today's workout, and every task, with the conscious understanding that you are actively shaping who you are becoming.

**Today's challenge:** Today, pick one movement you want to improve and consciously focus on perfect form for every single rep, treating it as a deliberate practice session.
