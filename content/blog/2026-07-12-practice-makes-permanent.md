## Practice Makes Permanent

> Every habit and faculty is confirmed and strengthened by the corresponding actions, that of walking by walking, that of running by running. If you wish to be a reader, read; if a writer, write. In the same way, if you wish to be strong, exercise.
>
> — Epictetus, Discourses 2.18.1

Think about the movements you perform daily, both in and out of the gym. Each rep, each step, each lift isn't just about finishing the workout; it's about engraving a pattern, reinforcing a habit. Your body and mind are constantly learning, becoming what you repeatedly do.

This isn't just about physical strength; it's about mental fortitude. Every time you push through a tough set, you're not just building muscle, you're building resilience. So, approach each session not as a one-off event, but as deliberate practice, shaping the person you are becoming.

**Today's challenge:** Today, pick one movement you find challenging and focus on perfect form for every single rep, treating it as a masterclass in skill, not just a workout.
