## The Value Of Hardship

> No man is more unhappy than he who never faces adversity; for he is not permitted to prove himself.
>
> — Seneca, On Providence 4.3

Today's WOD might feel like a wall, a relentless test of your limits. That burning sensation, the heavy breath, the doubt creeping in – these are not failures. They are the very moments where you get to prove what you're made of, where your character is forged under pressure.

Embrace the discomfort. It’s not just about physical strength; it's about mental fortitude. Every rep, every round, every struggle is an opportunity to show yourself what you can endure and overcome. Don't shy away from the hard parts; lean into them, for that is where true growth happens.

**Today's challenge:** When you hit a tough spot in today's workout, instead of backing off, consciously push for one more rep or five more seconds.
