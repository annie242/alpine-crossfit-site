## The Value Of Discomfort

> No man is more unhappy than he who never suffers adversity. For he has not had the opportunity to test himself.
>
> — Seneca, On Providence 4.4

Think about the last time you truly pushed past what felt comfortable. Maybe it was that final rep, or the last few meters of a run, or simply showing up when you really didn't want to. That moment of resistance, that prickle of pain or doubt, is where growth happens.

Without these challenges, we never truly know our limits or our strength. Embrace the discomfort today, not as something to avoid, but as the very forge that sharpens your resolve and reveals your true capabilities.

**Today's challenge:** During today's workout, identify one moment of discomfort and lean into it for just one extra second, knowing it's making you stronger.
