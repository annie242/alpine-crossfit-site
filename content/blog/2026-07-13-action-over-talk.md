## Action Over Talk

> Don't just say you have a good character, prove it by your actions.
>
> — Epictetus, Enchiridion 46

It's easy to talk about being strong, disciplined, or committed. We can tell ourselves we'll crush the next WOD, stick to our nutrition plan, or show up consistently. But the real measure isn't in the words we speak, it's in the sweat, the effort, and the choices we make when no one's watching.

Your character isn't defined by your intentions or your boasts, but by your consistent actions. Today, let your performance speak louder than any internal monologue or external declaration you might make.

**Today's challenge:** Before you leave the gym today, perform one extra rep, one extra set, or hold one position for an additional 10 seconds, simply because you can.
