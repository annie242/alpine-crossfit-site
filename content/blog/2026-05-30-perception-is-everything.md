## Perception Is Everything

> Men are disturbed not by things, but by the views which they take of them.
>
> — Epictetus, Enchiridion 5

That daunting WOD, the heavy barbell, or the complex movement isn't inherently 'hard' or 'impossible.' It's our internal narrative, the story we tell ourselves about it, that creates the struggle. The physical challenge is one thing; the mental battle is often far greater.

Today, recognize that your perception dictates your experience. You can choose to see an obstacle as a chance to grow, a heavy lift as an opportunity to prove your strength, or a skill as a puzzle to solve. Shift your perspective, and you shift your potential.

**Today's challenge:** Before your next set or movement, pause and consciously reframe any negative thought about it into a positive or neutral one.
