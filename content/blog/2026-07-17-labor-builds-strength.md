## Labor Builds Strength

> Difficulties strengthen the mind, as labor does the body.
>
> — Seneca, Moral Letters to Lucilius 13.4

Every rep, every WOD, every time you push past what you thought was possible — that's your body adapting, growing stronger. But Seneca reminds us that this principle isn't just for muscle. The mental grind, the moments of doubt, the commitment to show up even when you don't want to, these are the 'difficulties' that forge an unshakeable mind.

Just as consistent physical labor builds a resilient physique, consistent mental effort in the face of challenges builds an indomitable spirit. Don't shy away from the struggle; embrace it as the very mechanism for your growth. The discomfort you feel now is the raw material for your future strength, so lean into it.

**Today's challenge:** Today, when the workout gets tough, acknowledge the mental friction and consciously choose to push through one more rep, one more minute, with a clear and focused mind.
