## Build Your Inner Strength

> No man is free who is not master of himself.
>
> — Epictetus, Discourses 4.1.1

The weight on the bar, the clock ticking down, the burning in your lungs—these are external challenges. True mastery, however, begins within. It's about conquering your own doubts, your own impulses to quit, and the mental resistance that often feels heavier than any barbell.

Every rep, every WOD, every decision to show up is an opportunity to practice this self-mastery. You're not just building muscle; you're forging a resilient mind. Focus on controlling your effort and attitude, and you'll find freedom in every challenge.

**Today's challenge:** Today, when you feel like easing up, consciously push for one more rep or one more second of effort, just to prove you're in charge.
