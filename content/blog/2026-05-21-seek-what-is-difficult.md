## Seek What Is Difficult

> No man is more unhappy than he who never faces adversity. For he is not permitted to prove himself.
>
> — Seneca, On Providence 4.3

Think about the workouts that make you uncomfortable, the movements you dread, or the weights that feel too heavy. These aren't obstacles to avoid; they are opportunities. It's in these moments of friction that you discover what you're truly capable of, not just physically, but mentally.

Embracing the hard stuff builds resilience. It shows you that you can endure, adapt, and grow. Don't shy away from the challenges in the gym or in life; lean into them, because that's where your real strength is forged.

**Today's challenge:** Today, when you feel the urge to scale down or avoid a tough movement, choose the harder option instead, even if it's just for one rep.
