## Master Your Inner Self

> No man is free who is not master of himself.
>
> — Epictetus, Discourses 4.1.1

The weight on the bar, the clock ticking down, the burning lungs — these are external challenges. But the real battle, the one that determines whether you push through or give up, happens entirely within your own mind. It's the internal dialogue, the voice that whispers 'stop' or 'you can't.'

True freedom isn't about avoiding discomfort; it's about controlling your response to it. When you master your impulses, your doubts, and your fears, you unlock a power far greater than any physical strength. You become free to choose effort, free to embrace the grind, free to become who you're meant to be.

**Today's challenge:** Today, when you hit that uncomfortable spot in your workout, acknowledge the urge to quit, but then choose to push through for just one more rep, one more second, one more meter.
