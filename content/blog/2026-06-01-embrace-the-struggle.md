## Embrace The Struggle

> No man is more unhappy than he who never faces adversity, for he is not permitted to prove himself.
>
> — Seneca, On Providence 4.2

That moment when the WOD gets heavy, the reps pile up, or your lungs burn? That's not just discomfort; it's an invitation. It's the universe, or maybe just the programming, asking you to show up, to dig deep, and to prove what you're made of.

Don't shy away from the hard parts. These are the moments that forge resilience, clarify your limits, and ultimately expand them. Lean into the grind today, and you'll discover strength you didn't know you possessed.

**Today's challenge:** When you hit that wall today, instead of backing off, take one more breath, find one more rep, or push for five more seconds.
