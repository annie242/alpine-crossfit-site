## The Power Of Habit

> Every habit and faculty is corroborated and increased by the corresponding actions: the habit of walking by walking, the habit of running by running.
>
> — Epictetus, Discourses 2.18.1

Think about that first pull-up, that heavy deadlift, or even just showing up when you'd rather stay home. Each repetition, each decision to act, isn't just building muscle; it's forging a deeper groove in your mind. You are literally practicing the person you want to become.

Every time you choose discipline over comfort, you strengthen that internal muscle. Don't underestimate the quiet power of consistency. What you repeatedly do, you become. So, make your actions count, because you're building your future self, one rep at a time.

**Today's challenge:** Today, identify one small, positive habit you want to reinforce, and execute it with full intention, knowing you're building a stronger you.
