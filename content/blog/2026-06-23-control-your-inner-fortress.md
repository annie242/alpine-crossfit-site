## Control Your Inner Fortress

> If you are distressed by anything external, the pain is not due to the thing itself, but to your estimate of it; and this you have the power to revoke at any moment.
>
> — Marcus Aurelius, Meditations 8.47

That burning sensation in your lungs, the heavy barbell, the clock ticking down—these are just facts. They exist. But your reaction to them, the narrative you build around them, that's entirely within your control. You can choose to see them as insurmountable obstacles or as opportunities to prove your resilience.

Your mind is your ultimate training ground. It dictates whether you push through or give up. The external world will always present challenges, but your inner response is your own domain. Master that, and you master everything you face.

**Today's challenge:** Today, when a WOD movement feels particularly brutal, consciously reframe your internal dialogue from 'I can't' to 'I choose to endure this'.
