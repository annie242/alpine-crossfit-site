## Endure And Persevere

> No man is crushed by misfortune unless he has first been broken by prosperity.
>
> — Seneca, Letters from a Stoic 67.12

Today’s WOD might look daunting. Maybe it’s a heavy lift, a long conditioning piece, or a skill you haven’t quite mastered. It’s in these moments of struggle, not ease, that your true strength is forged. The weights don't care about your mood; the clock doesn't negotiate.

Stoics understood that true resilience isn't built when things are comfortable, but when you face adversity head-on. Don't shy away from the discomfort; lean into it. This is where you grow, where you prove to yourself what you're capable of.

**Today's challenge:** During today's toughest moment, consciously choose to dig a little deeper, even when your mind tells you to quit.
