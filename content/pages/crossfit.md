    <span class="eyebrow">The short answer</span>
    <h2>What is a CrossFit class at Alpine like?</h2>
    <p>Every Alpine CrossFit class is a 60-minute coached session with five parts:</p>
    <ul class="feature-list">
      <li><strong>Introduction (5 min):</strong> The coach explains the day's workout and talks through scaling options.</li>
      <li><strong>General warm-up (10 min):</strong> Movement prep and mobility work.</li>
      <li><strong>Strength or skill (15 min):</strong> Progressive barbell work or movement practice.</li>
      <li><strong>Workout of the Day (20 min):</strong> Timed or scored metabolic conditioning.</li>
      <li><strong>Cool-down (10 min):</strong> Recovery breathing, mobility, and accessory work.</li>
    </ul>
    <p>A CF-L2 certified coach is on the floor for the entire hour. Scaling — load, range of motion, complexity — happens in real time based on your body and your experience.</p>
  </div>
</section>

<section class="section section-dark">
  <div class="container container-narrow">
    <span class="eyebrow">Class size</span>
    <h2>How many people are in a class?</h2>
    <p>Every CrossFit class at Alpine is capped at <strong>15 athletes</strong>. That keeps every member on the coach's radar and ensures real individual attention, even in our busiest hours. You will never be one of fifty bodies in a gym-class style room.</p>
  </div>
</section>

<section class="section">
  <div class="container container-narrow">
    <span class="eyebrow">In-house programming</span>
    <h2>Who writes the workouts?</h2>
    <p>Every workout at Alpine is programmed in-house by Megan Markee — CSCS certified and a 2× CrossFit Games Semifinalist. Programming is constantly varied (true CrossFit methodology), not a franchise template, not a subscription service, and not a random WOD generator.</p>
    <p>Megan trains at Alpine every day. She writes the programming, and she does the workouts herself — training alongside the members who do them with her. That keeps the programming honest and scaled appropriately for the range of athletes in the room.</p>
  </div>
</section>

<section class="section section-dark">
  <div class="container container-narrow">
    <span class="eyebrow">Who it's for</span>
    <h2>Complete beginners through competitive athletes.</h2>
    <p>CrossFit classes at Alpine are scaled to every level. Your first class might look completely different from a competitive athlete's first class — and you would both be working at the right capacity for your body.</p>
    <p>Every movement has regression and progression options. Your coach scales weight, reps, range of motion, and complexity in real time. Beginners are not held back; advanced athletes are not held back. Everyone trains the same workout, scaled to their version of hard.</p>
  </div>
</section>
