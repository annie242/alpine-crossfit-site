    <span class="eyebrow">What is HYROX</span>
    <h2>A global fitness competition.</h2>
    <p>HYROX is an 8-kilometer obstacle racing event (8 stations of 1km runs separated by workout challenges). Athletes compete in male, female, and mixed-team categories, with qualifying rounds leading to international finals.</p>
    <p>It sits between CrossFit (strength and conditioning) and obstacle racing (running and agility) — and that's why Alpine is a perfect training ground.</p>

</section>
