    <span class="eyebrow">The race</span>
    <h2>What HYROX Denver 2026 actually is.</h2>
    <p>HYROX is a hybrid fitness race that's grown from a niche event to over 750,000 racers worldwide in 2025. Every race is identical: <strong>8 stations, 8 one-kilometer runs, one continuous clock.</strong> You run a kilometer, do a station, run a kilometer, do a station — eight times.</p>
    <p>The stations, in order:</p>
    <ol style="line-height:1.8;color:var(--color-text-muted);">
      <li><strong style="color:var(--color-text);">1000m SkiErg</strong></li>
      <li><strong style="color:var(--color-text);">50m Sled Push</strong></li>
      <li><strong style="color:var(--color-text);">50m Sled Pull</strong></li>
      <li><strong style="color:var(--color-text);">80m Burpee Broad Jumps</strong></li>
      <li><strong style="color:var(--color-text);">1000m Row</strong></li>
      <li><strong style="color:var(--color-text);">200m Farmers Carry</strong></li>
      <li><strong style="color:var(--color-text);">100m Sandbag Lunges</strong></li>
      <li><strong style="color:var(--color-text);">100 Wall Balls</strong></li>
    </ol>
    <p>Most first-time racers finish in 1:30 to 2:00. Pro division winners are under an hour. Either way, you're going to be running tired by station three — which is why HYROX-specific training matters.</p>

    <span class="eyebrow">Divisions</span>
    <h2 class="section-heading">Which division fits you.</h2>
    <div class="grid grid-2">
      <div class="card">
        <h3>HYROX Open</h3>
        <p>The standard division. Lower sled weights, smaller sandbag, scaled wall ball target. Most first-timers race Open. Available in Men, Women, and age-group categories from 25-29 through 70+.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <h3>HYROX Pro</h3>
        <p>Same race, heavier loads: 152 lb sled push (Men), 110 lb sled pull (Men), 30 lb sandbag, 20 lb wall ball. For experienced racers and athletes targeting podium finishes.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <h3>HYROX Doubles</h3>
        <p>Two teammates split the work — one runs while the other works the station, or you alternate reps. Same-gender (MM, WW) and Mixed (MX) categories.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <h3>HYROX Mixed Relay</h3>
        <p>4-person teams (2M + 2W) split the eight rounds. The most accessible format if you're new to racing but want the experience.</p>
      </div>
