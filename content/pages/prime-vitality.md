    <span class="eyebrow">The short answer</span>
    <h2>What is Prime Vitality?</h2>
    <p>Prime Vitality is Alpine CrossFit's Monday, Wednesday, and Friday 10 AM class, built specifically for adults 55 and older. It is real strength training — barbell work, functional movement, and measurable progression — scaled appropriately for the body you are training in today.</p>
    <p>Most senior fitness classes are low-impact cardio and light stretching. Prime Vitality is different. The goal is strength that translates: picking up grandchildren, hiking with ease, traveling without dreading airports, and staying independent as you age.</p>
  </div>
</section>

<section class="section section-dark">
  <div class="container container-narrow">
    <span class="eyebrow">The coach</span>
    <h2>Coached by a Masters competitor.</h2>
    <p>Prime Vitality is led by <strong>Lisa Arcangel</strong> — CF-L2 certified, 15+ years coaching, and a qualifier for the CrossFit Masters Semifinals. Lisa trains and competes at the level she coaches, in the 55+ age division. That matters: she is not teaching you a senior class, she is coaching you the same way she trains.</p>
  </div>
</section>

<section class="section">
  <div class="container container-narrow">
    <span class="eyebrow">What you do</span>
    <h2>What movements does Prime Vitality include?</h2>
    <ul class="feature-list">
      <li><strong>Barbell training</strong> — squats, deadlifts, presses, and pulls, with load that progresses as you do.</li>
      <li><strong>Functional movement</strong> — carries, lunges, step-ups, throws. The patterns of a strong life outside the gym.</li>
      <li><strong>Moderate-intensity conditioning</strong> — rowing, bike, sled — to build the aerobic engine that supports everything else.</li>
      <li><strong>Mobility and stability work</strong> — built into every session so range of motion improves alongside strength.</li>
    </ul>
    <p>Every movement scales — load, range of motion, and complexity all adjust to the individual.</p>
  </div>
</section>

<section class="section section-dark">
  <div class="container container-narrow">
    <span class="eyebrow">How it's different</span>
    <h2>How is this different from SilverSneakers or low-impact senior classes?</h2>
    <p>SilverSneakers and most rec-center senior classes are designed around mobility maintenance — low impact, light load, group cardio. That has real value, and many of our members do both.</p>
    <p>What you do not get there is real, progressive strength training. Prime Vitality does. You lift, you progress, you measure. Members track their squat, deadlift, and press numbers. Many hit personal records in their 60s and 70s that they have never hit before.</p>
  </div>
</section>
