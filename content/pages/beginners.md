<section class="section section-dark">
  <div class="container container-narrow">
    <span class="eyebrow">Foundations</span>
    <h2>Private one-on-one coaching.</h2>
    <p>Foundations is a series of private sessions (typically 3–5) where a CF-L2 coach teaches you the core CrossFit movements: squatting, deadlifting, pressing, pulling, and carries.</p>
    <p>No pressure. No crowds. No confusion about what you're supposed to be doing. You move at your pace, and your coach corrects technique in real time.</p>
    <p>You graduate from Foundations when your coach is confident you can safely and effectively execute the movements in a group setting.</p>
  </div>
</section>

<section class="section">
  <div class="container container-narrow">
    <span class="eyebrow">Why private first</span>
    <h2>Set yourself up for success.</h2>
    <p>Group classes are better when you already know the basics. You'll have confidence. You'll understand the coach's cues. You'll spend less mental energy on "what am I doing?" and more on effort and breathing.</p>
    <p>Foundations gives you that foundation. It's an investment in your first month that pays back in safety and confidence for years.</p>
  </div>
</section>
