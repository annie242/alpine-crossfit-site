Your first visit

Your first visit, minute by minute.

Most first-time visitors come for a free No Sweat Intro — 45 minutes with a coach. Here's what that actually looks like:

  1. Walk in. The coach on duty will greet you. You don't need to have registered, though a heads-up helps.
  2. Tour. Your coach walks you through the gym — main floor, equipment, locker rooms, wellness space.
  3. Conversation. You sit down and talk about your goals, training history, any limitations, and what you want to change.
  4. Recommendation. Based on that conversation, the coach recommends a starting point — membership, Foundations, or one-on-one personal training.
  5. No pressure. You leave with a clear path forward and no obligation. Many members come back for a second visit before signing up.
What to bring

What to bring.

Nothing special for a No Sweat Intro — you won't work out. For your first class once you start:

  • Workout clothes
  • Athletic shoes (flat-soled or trainer preferred; any athletic shoe is fine for day one)
  • Water bottle
  • Questions

We provide everything else.
