Pricing FAQ

Common pricing questions.

Is there an initiation or signup fee?
No. The price you see is the price you pay. No initiation fee, no annual fee, no equipment maintenance charge.
Is there a contract?
No. All memberships are month-to-month. Cancel anytime with 30 days written notice.
What's the No Sweat Intro?
A free 45-minute meeting with a coach. You'll tour the gym, talk through your goals, and we'll recommend the best starting point. Zero pressure and zero cost.
What's included in the $69 Open Gym tier?
Facility access, wellness center (sauna, cold plunge, compression, peptide therapy), and 24/7 entry. CrossFit classes and Prime Vitality are not included at this tier — those start at Unlimited.
How does the $999 Personal Training tier work?
12 one-on-one personal training sessions per month (roughly 3 per week), individualized programming built around your goals, plus everything in Unlimited — CrossFit classes, Prime Vitality, wellness center, and 24/7 access. It's designed for athletes with specific performance goals or anyone who wants the full coaching stack.
Can I freeze my membership?
Yes. Members can freeze for up to 90 days per year for travel, injury, or life events. No charge during a freeze.
Do you offer military, first responder, or student discounts?
Yes. Reach out and we'll share current offers.
How do I cancel?
Email us or tell your coach in person. 30 days notice and you're done — no cancellation fee.
What payment methods do you accept?
Credit card and ACH (bank draft). Memberships bill on the same date each month.

Still have questions?

The fastest way to figure out if Alpine is right for you is to come meet a coach in person. Free, 45 minutes, no pressure.
