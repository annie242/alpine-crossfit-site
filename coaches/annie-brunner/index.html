Annie Brunner.

Owner · CF-L2 · Former Pro Soccer Player · 4× CrossFit Quarterfinalist

Annie Brunner is a co-owner of Alpine CrossFit in Wheat Ridge, Colorado. A former professional soccer player and collegiate soccer coach, Annie trains at Alpine every single day alongside members — which is how the standard gets set. She competes annually at the CrossFit Games Quarterfinals, most recently qualifying in the 2026 season.

Credentials

  • CrossFit Level 2 Trainer (CF-L2)
  • Co-owner of Alpine CrossFit
  • Former professional soccer player
  • Former collegiate soccer coach
  • 4× CrossFit Games Quarterfinalist (2021, 2022, 2024, 2026)

As an athlete

Annie has competed in the CrossFit Open every season since 2016, placing consistently in the top tier worldwide among individual women. Before CrossFit, Annie played professional soccer and coached at the collegiate level.
