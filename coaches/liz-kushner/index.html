Liz Kushner.

Coach · CF-L2 · 10+ years coaching

Liz Kushner is a CrossFit Level 2 Trainer (CF-L2) at Alpine CrossFit with more than a decade of coaching experience. An active competitive athlete, Liz qualified for the 2026 CrossFit Quarterfinals in the Women's 35–39 age group and has coached out of multiple Denver-area affiliates before making Alpine her home. She's also Alpine's resident class clown — the coach whose presence makes every class memorable, whether she's dialing in a clean or cracking up the room mid-WOD.

Credentials

  • CrossFit Level 2 Trainer (CF-L2)
  • 10+ years coaching

As an athlete

Liz has competed in the CrossFit Open across multiple seasons, with significant year-over-year progression. In the 2026 season she qualified for Quarterfinals in the Women's 35–39 age group, finishing in the top tier of her age group across North America West and worldwide.
