Coaches & Owners

Three CF-L2 coaches. Two owner-athletes. One standard.

Every class at Alpine is led by one of three CF-L2 certified coaches. Our two owners — both active CrossFit competitors — don't coach group classes. They train at Alpine every day alongside members, which is how the standard gets set and how the programming stays honest.
