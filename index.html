Who Alpine is for

Not another big-box gym.

Most gyms in the Denver metro are built for people who already know what they're doing. Alpine is built for people who want a coach, a crew, and a workout that actually gets them somewhere — whether that's dropping a jean size, playing with their kids without getting winded, or squatting more than they did in college.

We coach every class. Every workout scales to your body and your experience. And nobody trains alone.

Getting started

Getting started is easy.

1. Book a free No Sweat Intro.

Come meet a coach for a 45-minute chat. We'll tour the gym, talk through your goals, and figure out the right starting point together. No pressure. No cost.

2. Try a class.

Once we've matched you to the right program — CrossFit, personal training, or Prime Vitality for members 55+ — we'll get you into your first coached session.

3. Start training.

Sign up for the membership that fits. Show up. We'll handle the rest.

Common questions

Common questions.

Do I need to be in shape before I start?
No. Most new Alpine members start from scratch or come back after years away. Every class scales to your body. Your coach will never throw you into something you can't handle.
How much does a CrossFit membership cost?
Memberships at Alpine range from $69/month (Open Gym: facility, wellness, 24/7) to $199/month (Unlimited: classes, wellness center, Prime Vitality, and 24/7 access). See full pricing →
What is a No Sweat Intro?
A free 45-minute meeting with a coach. You'll tour the gym, talk through your goals, and we'll recommend the best starting point. No pressure, no cost.
Is there a contract?
No. All memberships are month-to-month. Cancel with 30 days written notice.
Where are you located?
